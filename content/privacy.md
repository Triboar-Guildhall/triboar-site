**Last Updated: November 30, 2024**

## Our Commitment to Privacy

At Triboar Guildhall, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the data we collect through our website and Discord community.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you join the Guild, we collect:

- **Account Information**: Discord username, email address
- **Payment Information**: Payment details processed through our payment processor (we do not store credit card information)
- **Character Information**: Character names, backstories, and game-related content you create
- **Communications**: Messages and content you share in our Discord server

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with our services
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, IP address
- **Cookies**: We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the Triboar Guildhall community
- Process membership dues and manage subscriptions
- Facilitate gameplay and storytelling activities
- Communicate with you about community updates, quests, and events
- Ensure community safety and enforce our guidelines
- Improve our services and develop new features

## Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

- **Service Providers**: Discord, payment processors, and hosting services that help us operate
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
- **Community Members**: Character information and in-game content you choose to share

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and personal information at any time by contacting us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt-out of non-essential communications
- Export your character and story content

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

The Triboar Guildhall is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18.

## Third-Party Services

Our services integrate with Discord and payment processors. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them:

- [Discord Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy)
- Payment processor privacy policies (as applicable)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through Discord or email. Your continued use of our services after changes indicates acceptance.
