Our Promise on Fairness

One price, one community. At Triboar Guildhall, every member pays the same dues and receives the same access — no tiers, no pay-to-win.

At the Triboar Guildhall, membership is simple and equitable. Every adventurer pays the same dues and receives the same access — no tiers, no boosts, no hidden upgrades. Your character’s story is shaped by your choices and your writing, not your wallet.

Why We Do It This Way

One Price, No Tiers

Everyone contributes equally to the world we’re building. There are no premium levels, paid advantages, or “VIP players.” Every member stands on the same ground.

Unlimited Access for Every Member

Your membership includes unlimited quests, roleplay scenes, and downtime activities. Join missions whenever you’re ready, write at your own pace, and build your character’s story freely.

No Pay-to-Play — and No Pay-to-Win

Gold, XP, and items must be earned through storytelling and gameplay. You can’t buy power here — every achievement is the result of your choices and your creativity.

Flexible, Pause-Anytime Membership

Membership is month-to-month, with the ability to pause whenever life gets busy. No long-term commitments, no penalties.

What You Get

A Quest Guarantee

We guarantee that there will always be at least one quest available to join every month. If, for some reason, we cannot offer you a quest that month, your next month is free.

Attentive Moderation & Support

Our moderation team helps keep the Guildhall safe, welcoming, and creatively rich. If you have questions, concerns, or need help navigating the world or tools, staff are available to assist.

Tools That Support You

Play-by-post tools — especially Avrae — can be complex at first. We provide clear guides, templates, and optional assistance so you can focus on writing and play, not fumbling with commands.

Alternate Ways to Contribute

Dungeon Masters

DMs receive free membership in exchange for running quests in the shared world. We provide scheduling support, worldbuilding input, and a collaborative community of fellow storytellers.
Apply to DM

Volunteers

Members may earn dues credit by helping with moderation, maps, rules upkeep, lore archiving, or other community needs. These roles are contributions, not premium tiers — and never grant in-game advantages.
