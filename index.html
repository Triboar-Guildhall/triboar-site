Why Guild Dues?

Think of it as a guild membership, not a subscription: your share sustains the community where your stories thrive.

FAQ

We're a play-by-post (PbP) roleplaying community set in the Forgotten Realms, using the D&D 5e 2024 ruleset. Our focus is on collaborative storytelling and immersive writing.

Instead of live voice sessions, adventures unfold through written posts in Discord. Each player writes their character's actions and dialogue, while Dungeon Masters guide the story forward.

Join our Discord, introduce yourself, review the Guild Charter, and contribute dues. Once approved, you'll gain access to campaigns, character creation, and community writing channels.

Dues keep the Guildhall sustainable by covering server costs, resources, maps, and community events. They ensure the stories we build together have a strong foundation.
