Triboar Guildhall Charter

A Community of Collaborative Storytelling

Purpose

The Triboar Guildhall is a play-by-post Westmarches community set in a persistent, evolving Forgotten Realms. We bring players together around collaborative storytelling, fair and equal access, respectful interactions, and creative play on your own schedule. This charter outlines our values, expectations, and commitments.

Core Values

Collaboration

We build stories together, listening and responding thoughtfully to fellow players and DMs.

Respect

Everyone is treated with kindness and dignity. Discrimination, harassment, or hostile behavior is never tolerated.

Fairness

No pay-to-win, no special treatment. All players receive the same opportunities, regardless of membership length or experience.

Creativity

The Guildhall supports imaginative writing, thoughtful character work, and shared worldbuilding.

Consistency

Our world persists. Your actions matter. We respect pacing, timelines, and the work of others.

Flexibility

Life happens. Asynchronous play allows for participation on your schedule, and our rules accommodate real-world needs.

Membership

Equal Access

All members receive:

  • Unlimited quests
  • Unlimited roleplay scenes
  • Full downtime system access
  • Attentive moderation and support
  • Tools and guides to assist with PBP play and Avrae

Dues & Contributions

Month-to-month, equal for all.

DMs play free. Volunteers earn credit.

Quest Guarantee

At least one quest you can join each month.

If we can't deliver, your next month is free.

Code of Conduct

The Guildhall thrives on trust and communication. All members are expected to:

1

Engage Respectfully

Treat players, DMs, and staff with respect and kindness. This includes:

  • •No harassment or hate speech
  • •No discrimination (race, gender, orientation, disability, religion, etc.)
  • •Inclusive language and respectful storytelling
  • •PG-13 content limits (no ERP; avoid excessive gore)
  • •Use content warnings and spoiler tags for sensitive topics
2

Maintain IC/OOC Boundaries

  • •Use designated in-character and out-of-character channels appropriately
  • •Brief OOC notes in IC channels should be tagged: (OOC: …) or [[OOC: …]]
  • •Do not break immersion intentionally or derail scenes with OOC chatter
3

Respect DM Authority

Dungeon Masters guide scenes and adjudicate rules. If you disagree with a ruling, discuss it politely in private or OOC after the moment, not during play.

4

Player Consent is Required

You may not control, harm, or alter another player's character without explicit OOC consent. This includes:

  • •Forced movement
  • •Charm/domination effects
  • •PvP actions
  • •Narrative consequences affecting another player

Always check first.

5

Practice Fair Play

  • •No metagaming: Do not act on information your character doesn’t have
  • •No god-modding: Do not dictate another character’s actions or outcomes
  • •No power-gaming: Avoid exploiting mechanics to overshadow others

Our goal is rich storytelling, not mechanical domination.

6

Respect Pacing Requirements

Our play-by-post quests move at a steady pace so everyone can enjoy the story.

  • •During active quests, please respond within 24 hours
  • •If you need more time, communicate with your group or DM — we’re flexible if you speak up
  • •In combat or time-sensitive moments, if you miss the 24-hour window, your character may take a default action (usually Dodge)
  • •Repeated pacing issues may result in being removed from that quest, but never punished outside it

This protects momentum and honors everyone's time.

7

Use Channels Appropriately

  • •Post in the correct channels
  • •Keep memes, jokes, or unrelated chatter in OOC areas
  • •No NSFW, political debates, or inflammatory content
  • •No advertising or soliciting without admin approval
8

Leaving or Taking a Break

Pausing Membership

Pausing your subscription is always allowed and requires no staff approval. When you pause:

  • •You remain in the server
  • •You keep your character records
  • •You retain access to general community channels
  • •You simply lose access to quests, roleplay, and downtime activities until you resume

Pausing is part of our membership guarantee — use it whenever you need to.

Leaving the Discord Server

Leaving the server should only be done with notification to staff. If you leave without notice:

  • •Your characters and progress may be archived or deleted
  • •You may forfeit active characters and rewards

We cannot hold character records indefinitely for players who depart without communication.

Health & Personal Leave

If you need an extended break for personal or health reasons, message the moderators. We can place your characters on Health Leave, preserving their records until you’re ready to return.

Support & Moderation

We aim to maintain a constructive, safe, and enjoyable community.

Moderation Philosophy

Educate first. Correct behavior when needed. Prioritize community safety. Remove harmful actors quickly.

Enforcement

Violations of Respect or Consent may result in immediate removal. Other issues typically receive a warning first. Repeated problems or hostility toward staff may result in suspension or a ban.

Getting Help

If you encounter issues — rules questions, technical challenges, interpersonal concerns — contact the moderation team or admins. We are here to help.

A Living Community

This Charter reflects our ongoing commitment to fairness, creativity, and shared storytelling. As the Guildhall grows, we may refine or expand this Charter — always with transparency and community well-being in mind.
